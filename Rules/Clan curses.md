## Eternal Hunger

Clan members suffer from constant hunger.
During each rest phase, the clan consumes 1 additional food.
## Cursed Blood

The first time a goblin bleeds during a scouting or raid phase, a foul odor emanates from it for about ten meters until the next victory phase.
## Iron Haunts Us

Sometimes spirits manifest when a goblin from the clan touches a metal object. The larger the object, the more powerful and evil the spirit.
## Shadow of the Great Eater

The Great Eater pursues goblins into their nightmares. Sometimes they think they see him out of the corner of their eye and are paralyzed with fear.
